Why Recally works

A better way to learn English than isolated word lists.

Recally keeps words inside meaningful stories. You read, listen, tap unknown vocabulary, check translations, and review the same words later with small, repeatable sessions.

Read by level

Listen as you read

Tap for translations

Review with quizzes

Daily review and mini quizzes help convert new vocabulary into long-term memory.

Inside the app

Real screens that show the learning flow.

The product journey is straightforward: discover a book, read with context, save new words, then test yourself. This structure is also what makes the page easier to rank for intent-driven searches around reading practice and vocabulary building.

What learners can do in one session

  • Open a graded story matched to your current English level.
  • Tap an unfamiliar word and see its meaning without breaking flow.
  • Add useful words to a deck and review them with quick quizzes.
  • Continue improving pronunciation with audio and narrator settings.

Yes. The settings screens show translation language selection, which helps learners use the app with their preferred support language.
