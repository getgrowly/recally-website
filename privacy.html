Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Recallly. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered flashcard mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using Recallly:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account, or authentication data when using Google or Apple Sign-In
  • Flashcard Content: Cards, decks, text, and images you create or upload for studying
  • Learning Data: Your study history, review results, spaced repetition intervals, and mastery levels
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, including features used, session duration, and study patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • AI Processing Data: Text and images submitted for AI card generation (processed but not stored beyond card creation)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our flashcard and spaced repetition services
  • To power AI-based card generation from text and images
  • To calculate optimal review intervals using our SRS algorithm
  • To sync your decks and progress across devices
  • To send you notifications about due reviews and study reminders
  • To track and display your learning progress and streaks
  • To improve and optimize our app and AI features
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our platform

AI and Third-Party Services

Recallly uses AI services to power features like automatic card generation:

  • OpenAI: Text and images submitted for AI card generation may be processed by OpenAI's API. This data is used solely for generating flashcard content and is subject to OpenAI's data usage policies.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase for authentication, cloud storage, and real-time sync. Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics services to understand usage patterns and improve our app.

We do not use your personal flashcard content to train AI models. Data sent for AI processing is used only to generate your requested cards.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud hosting, AI services, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. All user data is encrypted at rest and in transit. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Your flashcard content and learning data are retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your information
  • Data portability (export your decks and cards)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@recallly.app

Children's Privacy

Recallly is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States where our service providers operate. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Offline Mode and Local Storage

Recallly supports offline study mode. When offline, your study progress is stored locally on your device and automatically synced to our servers when you reconnect. You can also create local backups of your data for personal use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. For material changes, we will notify you through the app or via email. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@recallly.app

Support: support@recallly.app
